The AI Timeline

From theoretical concept to world-changing technology - the evolution of artificial intelligence.

1950

The Birth of AI

Alan Turing proposes the "Turing Test" and considers the question "Can machines think?" laying the philosophical foundations for AI.

1997

Deep Blue vs. Kasparov

IBM's Deep Blue becomes the first computer system to defeat a reigning world chess champion under standard time controls.

2011

Siri & Watson

Apple introduces Siri as an integrated iPhone feature, while IBM's Watson defeats human champions at Jeopardy!, showcasing natural language understanding.

2016

AlphaGo Breakthrough

DeepMind's AlphaGo defeats world champion Lee Sedol in Go, a game considered vastly more complex than chess, marking a milestone in AI development.

2023

Generative AI Explosion

GPT-4 and other large language models demonstrate human-like conversation abilities, while AI image generators like DALL-E 3 produce photorealistic images from text descriptions, democratizing AI capabilities.
